Diego Armando Maradona

1960 - 2020

Biography

Diego Armando Maradona is considered one of the best football players of all time. He was born in 1960 and was brought up in the Lanus neighbourhood of Buenos Aires. Maradona started to play football in the youth categories of the Argentina Juniors football club. He made his debut with the first team in 1976 when he was just 15 years old, and, two years later, in 1978, he made his debut with the Argentinian national team. In 1981 Maradona joined Boca Juniors, one of the most famous football clubs in Argentina. In 1986 he won the football World Cup with the Argentinian national team.
